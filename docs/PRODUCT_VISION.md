# Safety as a Contact: Product Vision

## Overview

Safety as a Contact is an SMS-based behavioral safety coaching platform for construction. It shifts the industry from compliance-driven safety (Safety I) to learning-driven safety (Safety II) through a feedback loop that workers can feel.

## The Problem

Construction kills 1,069 workers annually (2022 BLS). Traditional compliance-based safety treats workers as problems to control rather than partners in risk mitigation. Behavioral safety programs are hard to implement in transient workforces. Higher Order of Precaution (HOP) lacks widespread adoption. The industry is losing experienced workers to retirement, taking decades of institutional safety knowledge with them.

## The Paradigm Shift

**From compliance to coaching.** From data collection to behavior change. From unread dashboards to a feedback loop workers can feel.

Safety as a Contact integrates Safety II principles (learning from everyday success) with behaviorally validated interventions delivered via SMS. The feedback loop **is** the product:

Worker observes hazard → texts it → gets coaching response → observation feeds toolbox talk → worker sees their input had impact → reports more.

## Why SMS

- 74% of people read all texts; 62% within minutes
- No app download, no login, no training needed — universal access on any phone
- Two-way messaging shows higher intervention effects than one-way delivery
- Personalized, targeted SMS interventions show effect size d=0.442 (Cohen's medium)
- Removes the #1 adoption barrier in construction: workers don't download apps
- Push delivery — arrives without user effort

## Five Coaching Response Modes

Each text message is a coaching opportunity, not data collection:

### ALERT
Immediate hazard recognition. Urgent, clear, unambiguous.
> "That's a serious fall risk. Here's what OSHA 1926.501 requires..."

### VALIDATE
Positive reinforcement of good catches.
> "Good eye. That tripping hazard near the south entrance is exactly the kind of thing that prevents incidents."

### NUDGE
Gentle coaching toward deeper observation.
> "You noticed the scaffolding issue. What about the guardrails — did they look secure?"

### PROBE
Questions that build observational skill.
> "What did your gut tell you when you saw that? Trust that instinct."

### AFFIRM
Empowerment and self-efficacy building.
> "Your crew's safer because you spoke up. That's what it's about."

## The Feedback Loop (Core Product Mechanism)

1. **Worker observations** flow into toolbox talks — workers see their observations become group discussion topics
2. **Social proof**: Reporting is valued and visible
3. **Foreman receives coaching prompts** (not scripts) based on crew observations
4. **Proactive shift-start nudges** contextualized to worker's trade and project
5. **Safety Engagement Score** tracks behavioral change, not checkbox compliance

This is what makes the platform "a contact" — it's tangible, immediate, and bidirectional.

## Key Personas

### THE WORKER
Primary user. Texts observations from the field. Needs zero friction, immediate validation, anonymity option.

### THE FOREMAN
Key adoption persona. Receives coaching prompts, toolbox talk content, crew engagement insights. Empowered to lead safety conversations with psychology, not KPIs.

### THE SAFETY PROFESSIONAL
Shifts from clipboard cop to coaching facilitator. Portal shows narrative intelligence, not dashboards.

### THE COMPANY ADMIN
Configures the system per-company. Uploads standards, manages projects, views engagement metrics.

## Bilingual by Design

Construction's workforce is diverse. A significant percentage of workers are Spanish-speaking, and many are more comfortable communicating in Spanish. Safety as a Contact must support Spanish from early implementation — not as a Phase 6 afterthought, but as a core accessibility feature by Phase 3. If behavioral science says removing barriers increases behavior, language is the next barrier after friction. The coaching engine must detect language preference and respond accordingly. Consent flows, opt-out processing, and compliance messages must work in both English and Spanish.

## What We Are NOT

- Not a surveillance tool
- Not a compliance checkbox system
- Not a dashboard platform
- Not a punishment mechanism
- Not an app that needs downloading

## Guiding Principles

- **Worker experience first.** Always.
- **Every text is a coaching opportunity**, never just data collection.
- **The feedback loop must be visible and fast.**
- **Anonymity is a feature, not a bug.**
- **The foreman is the force multiplier.**
- **Trade-specific knowledge earns trust.**
- **Simple enough for day one, deep enough for year five.**
- **Compliance is a byproduct of good culture, not the goal.**
- **If it feels like a corporate safety program, we've failed.**
- **The best safety program is one that actually listens.**
