# Frontend and Brand Rules

Rules for frontend and brand consistency:

## Color Palette
- Safety Amber: #E8922A (primary accent, CTAs)
- Deep Slate: #1E2A3B (text, secondary elements)
- Concrete: #F2F0EC (backgrounds, neutral)
- Field Green: #3A7D44 (success states, positive actions)
- Iron: #4A4A4A (secondary text, muted elements)
- Caution Red: #C0392B (warnings/errors ONLY, never for CTAs)

## Color Distribution Ratio
- 60% Concrete/White backgrounds
- 25% Deep Slate
- 10% Safety Amber
- 5% accents and Field Green

## Typography
- Headlines: DM Sans (bold, weight 700) — load from Google Fonts
- Body text: Inter (regular, weight 400) — load from Google Fonts
- Minimum body text: 16px
- Line height: 1.5
- Maximum line length: 75 characters — break into columns if wider

## Brand Voice and Language
- Direct, warm, knowledgeable, second person
- Use contractions and short sentences
- Use active voice
- Use trade language appropriate to audience
- Use "crew, team, hands" not "workforce, personnel"

## Accessibility and Responsiveness
- WCAG 2.1 AA minimum compliance
- Maintain proper contrast ratios
- Include alt text for all images
- Use semantic HTML
- All pages must be mobile-responsive (workers use phones)
- Test on small screens and touch devices

## Required Pages and Footer
- Privacy Policy and Terms pages must include all SMS compliance language
- Include SMS terms link in footer of every page
- All CTAs use Safety Amber background with Deep Slate text
